# ReportIQ Agency Client Acquisition Playbook
*A 30–60 Day Plan for Outreaching, Finding Leads, and Landing Recurring Clients*

This is where the money is. If you do this consistently for 30–60 days, you'll give yourself a real chance of landing recurring clients.

---

*Don't email everyone. Only target agencies that already have clients and likely need recurring reporting.*

### Priority 1 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

- Digital Marketing Agencies
- PPC Agencies

### Priority 2 ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆

### Priority 3 ⭐⭐⭐☆☆

## 🔍 Where to Find Agencies

### 1. Google Search
Search queries like:
```text
SEO agency Austin
Digital marketing agency Chicago
PPC agency Toronto
Web design agency London
SEO agency Sydney
```

### 2. Clutch.co
Search by categories: **SEO, PPC, Digital Marketing**. Look for agencies with **2–50 employees**. These mid-sized agencies are the best fit for outsourced reporting support.

### 3. LinkedIn
Search for titles:
- `SEO Agency Owner`
- `Founder Digital Marketing Agency`
- `Marketing Agency Owner`
- `Managing Director SEO`
Connect directly with the owners.

### 4. Agency Directories
Use platforms like **DesignRush** and **GoodFirms** to quickly build lead lists.

## 📧 Email Subject Lines
*Rotate them instead of using the same one every time to test open rates.*

- Quick question about your client reporting
- White-label reporting support
- Helping agencies save reporting time
- Reporting support for your agency
- A quick idea for your reporting workflow
- Monthly client reporting
- White-label reporting partner
- Helping with client reporting
- Agency reporting support
- Quick introduction

---

## ✉️ Cold Email Template
*Use this as a starting point. Make sure to personalize the first sentence for each agency.*

> **Subject:** Quick question about your client reporting
>
> Hi {{First Name}},
>
> I came across {{Agency Name}} and noticed you work with SEO and digital marketing clients.
> 
> I help agencies save time by handling white-label client reporting and operations behind the scenes. Instead of spending hours preparing monthly reports and client updates, I create professional, branded deliverables that your team can send directly to clients.
>
> Some of the work I can handle includes:
> - Monthly SEO reports
> - PPC performance reports
> - Weekly client updates
> - Client update emails
> - Client onboarding documents
> - Scope of Work (SOW) documents
>
> Everything is white-label, so it looks like it came directly from your agency.
> 
> If reporting is taking time away from strategy and client work, I'd be happy to discuss how I can support your team.
>
> Best,  
> Muhammad  
> Founder, ReportScale Operations

---

## 💡 Soft Call-to-Action (CTA)
Don't ask for a big commitment initially:
- **Avoid:** "Would you like to subscribe?"
- **Use:** "If it makes sense, I'd be happy to create a sample report for one of your clients so you can evaluate the quality."

*This is a much smaller, friction-free ask.*

---

## 🎯 Your First Milestone
Don't stress about making $10,000/month yet. Work step-by-step:
- **Agency #1:** Deliver excellent work.
- **Agency #2:** Refine and optimize your workflow.
- **Agency #3:** Ask for a testimonial and referral.

Three satisfied agencies will teach you more about what to build into ReportIQ than months of guessing. Their real feedback is what will make your software product highly valuable.
