# ReportIQ Agency Pricing Playbook
*A Guide on Setting Prices and Offering Options Before Pitching to Clients*

This is something you should decide **before** talking to clients. I recommend using **both per-report pricing and monthly retainers**.

The reason is simple:
- Some agencies want to **try you first** (lower risk).
- Others already know they need ongoing help.

By offering both options, you remove friction and make it easier for them to get started.

---

## Option 1 — Per Report (Entry Offer)
*This gets you in the door. These are easier for a new client to say "yes" to as a trial.*

| Service | Suggested Price |
| :--- | :---: |
| **Monthly SEO Report** | $49 – $79 |
| **Monthly PPC Report** | $49 – $79 |
| **Weekly Client Report** | $20 – $35 |
| **Client Update Email** | $10 – $20 |
| **Client Onboarding Package** | $40 – $75 |
| **Scope of Work (SOW)** | $30 – $60 |
| **Proposal Document** | $30 – $60 |

---

## Option 2 — Monthly Retainers (Main Goal)
*Once they like your trial work, move them to a monthly package for recurring revenue.*

### 🚀 Starter — $199/month
- Up to **5 reports**
- Weekly updates
- Client emails

### ⭐ Growth — $399/month *(Recommended Main Package)*
- Up to **10 reports**
- Weekly updates
- Client emails
- Client onboarding
- SOWs

### 📈 Scale — $699/month
- Up to **20 reports**
- Priority turnaround
- All agency operations documents

---

## How to Answer When They Ask

If a client asks:
> **"What's your pricing?"**

You can reply:
> "I offer both one-time and monthly options.
> 
> For agencies wanting to try the service first, I can create individual white-label reports starting from $49 per report.
> 
> For ongoing support, most agencies prefer a monthly package. Those typically range from $199 to $699 per month depending on the number of clients, reports, and turnaround requirements.
> 
> If you can tell me roughly how many clients you manage each month, I can recommend the most cost-effective option."

---

## If They Say "We Have 20 Clients"
*Don't immediately quote the $699 package price.*

First, ask clarifying questions:
1. How many reports do you send each month?
2. SEO only, or SEO + PPC?
3. Weekly updates too?
4. Do you already have report templates?

**Then give them a custom, tailored quote.**

---

## Should You Charge Per Client or Per Report?
I recommend **per report**, because not every client receives the same number of deliverables.

For example:
- **Agency A** has 10 clients but only needs monthly SEO reports.
- **Agency B** has 10 clients and wants monthly SEO reports, monthly PPC reports, weekly updates, and client emails.

Agency B requires much more work, so charging by deliverables is fairer and more profitable.

---

## My Recommendation for Your First 3 Months
Don't try to maximize your prices right away. Your priorities should be:
1. Win your first client.
2. Deliver excellent work.
3. Get a testimonial.
4. Convert them into a recurring monthly retainer.

Once you have a few successful case studies, you can gradually increase your pricing for new clients.
